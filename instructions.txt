Instructions on banana bread:

1. Pre-heat oven at 350 degrees
2. While oven is warming up, prepare and butter a 8 x 4 inch loaf pan
3. Mash bananas and add butter
4. Stir in a mixing bowl, and mash ripe bananas with fork until smooth
5. Stir melted butter into the mashed bananas
6. Mix in the remaining ingredients:
  - baking soda
  - salt
  - egg
  - vanilla extract
  - flour
  
  
7. Pour batter into the loaf pan
8. Bake for 55-65 minutes at 350 degress
9. Cool and serve

Banana bread is done!!
